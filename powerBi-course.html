Course Overview

Master the art of data visualization and business intelligence with Power BI and Tableau.

This comprehensive course will teach you to transform raw data into compelling visual stories that drive business decisions. You'll learn both Power BI and Tableau to become proficient in the industry's leading BI tools.

What You'll Learn

  • Data connection and transformation
  • Creating interactive visualizations
  • Dashboard design principles
  • Advanced calculations and DAX
  • Sharing and collaboration features
  • Real-world business case studies
